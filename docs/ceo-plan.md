# CEO Plan: TerraDiagnosis / 地脉·镜

Generated by /plan-ceo-review on 2026-05-23
Branch: main | Mode: SCOPE EXPANSION (+5 features, vision items deferred)

## Hackathon Constraints
- Duration: ~48 hours
- Team: 1-2 builders
- Target: Desktop Chrome/Edge, WebGL 2.0
- Scaffold budget: 2 hours (Vite + R3F + Zustand + Worker setup)
- Build budget: < 3MB gzipped

## Vision

### 10x Check
12 个月后，TerraDiagnosis 是一个多人共享的星球创作平台——两个用户同时在同一颗星球上堆沙。锁定后 AI 文明引擎接管：山脉阻挡军队，河流交汇处长出城市，贸易路线沿海岸线延伸。用户导出星球档案，成为 TTRPG 地图、小说设定、或社交媒体上的世界观 OC。

### Platonic Ideal
第一次打开时，一颗裸星球缓缓旋转。手指放上去，地形起来。等高线像涟漪扩散。锁定后——云层移动，季风显现，河流从雪山融化流下，迎风坡森林变深绿。这不是「做了个地形」，这是创造了一个活的地方。

## Scope Decisions

| # | Proposal | Effort | Decision | Reasoning |
|---|----------|--------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | Multiplayer terrain editing | L | DEFERRED | Needs backend; prove single-player first |
| 2 | Terrain presets | S | ACCEPTED | 30 min for max demo impact |
| 3 | Weather particle effects | M | ACCEPTED | Makes simulation feel alive |
| 4 | Share URL (hash-encoded heightmap) | S | ACCEPTED | Pure frontend, demo shareable |
| 5 | Undo/redo (20-step ring buffer) | S | ACCEPTED | Creative confidence |
| 6 | Animated water shader | S | ACCEPTED | Cheap, transformative |

## Accepted Scope
- Terrain presets: "volcanic island", "mountain chain", "crater lake", "archipelago"
- Weather particles: rain, snow, wind in observation mode
- Share URL: LZ-String compressed 128×128 8-bit heightmap
- Undo/redo: 20-action ring buffer, snapshot on pointer-up
- Animated water shader: normal-map waves + depth gradient

## Deferred
- Multiplayer (needs backend/CRDT)
- AI civilization simulation
- VR/AR mode
- Day/night cycle
- Ambient audio
- Mobile touch

## Technical Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Share URL exceeds browser limit | High | 128×128 + 8-bit quantization → ~2KB base64 |
| Water shader + particles GPU budget | Med | 2000 particles cap; single noise sample |
| Undo buffer overfill | Med | Snapshot on pointer-up only |
| Greenfield scaffolding | Low | 2-hour budget; Vite template |
